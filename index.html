Platform Highlights

Why SentinelIQ Exists

UK SMEs manage governance through fragmented tools — spreadsheets, emails, and shared drives — creating invisible risk. SentinelIQ converts daily governance events into structured, quantifiable intelligence so leaders can act before problems escalate.

Policy Accountability Engine

Distribute internal policies digitally, track employee acknowledgements in real time, and receive automated escalation alerts when acknowledgement thresholds are missed. Audit-ready coverage reports generated instantly.

Supplier Reliability Engine

Log and categorise supplier issues — delivery delays, quality failures, communication breakdowns. A weighted reliability scoring model detects emerging patterns before they cause operational disruption.

Governance Health Score

A composite 0–100 score combining policy acknowledgement coverage, supplier reliability, open issue count, and response time into a single, colour-coded risk indicator: Healthy (green), At Risk (amber), Critical (red).

Risk Signal Processing

Raw governance data is converted into directional risk indicators. Three delayed supplier deliveries in 60 days becomes a risk alert, not a data point. Logic applied continuously and automatically — no manual analysis required.

Audit-Ready Evidence Export

Generate timestamped, immutable governance reports in minutes — policy version history, acknowledgement logs, supplier issue records, and escalation timelines bundled into a formatted evidence pack for audits and disputes.

SME-First Design

Dashboard-first routing, minimal-click core workflows, and colour-coded risk indicators mean an operations manager with no compliance background can understand their governance posture in under 10 minutes. No specialist required.

02 · SUPPLIER RELIABILITY ENGINE

Early Warning Intelligence on Supplier Risk

The Supplier Reliability Engine enables structured logging of supplier issues categorised by type, severity, and status. A weighted scoring model — informed by Lean Six Sigma process methodology — converts issue frequency, recency, and severity into a Supplier Reliability Score. Risk alerts are triggered automatically when scores breach defined thresholds.

  • Structured issue logging: delivery delays, quality failures, communication failures, contractual breaches
  • Delivery timeliness monitor — tracks expected versus actual delivery dates across the supplier portfolio
  • Weighted reliability scoring engine — recency-weighted, severity-adjusted per supplier
  • Risk alert engine — automatic amber and red alerts when reliability scores decline below set limits
  • Supplier profile service — centralised onboarding, categorisation, and contact management
