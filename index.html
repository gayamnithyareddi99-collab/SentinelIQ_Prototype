Core AI Models & Capabilities

Seven purpose-built intelligence modules — from rule-based scoring to ML-powered prediction

Governance Health Scoring Engine
Proprietary Weighted Algorithm · Live
Active

The core AI engine aggregates five normalised governance signals into a composite 0–100 Governance Health Score. Each input is weighted by sector-configurable importance coefficients and recency decay functions — ensuring recent events carry greater weight than historical ones.

Model Inputs
Policy acknowledgement coverage % Open supplier issue count Supplier reliability score (portfolio avg) Issue response time (hrs) Recency weighting factor
GHS = Σ(wᵢ × normalise(inputᵢ) × recencyDecay(tᵢ)) → [0,100]
● Healthy (70–100)
● At Risk (40–69)
● Critical (0–39)
Supplier Reliability Scoring Model
Multi-Variable FMEA-Derived Algorithm · Live
Active

Inspired by Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) methodology from co-founder Ravirajsinh's engineering background. Each supplier receives a continuous reliability score (0–100) based on issue frequency, severity classification, delivery timeliness, and response-to-escalation speed.

Scoring Variables
Issue frequency (rolling 60d) Severity weight (1–5 scale) Delivery delta (expected vs actual) Escalation response time Recency decay coefficient
SRS = 100 − Σ(severityᵢ × frequencyWeight × recencyDecay(tᵢ))
Score <40 → Auto risk alert triggered
3+ issues in 60 days → Pattern escalation
Score ≥75 → Reliable supplier status
Policy Risk Signal Classifier
Rule-Based Signal Engine · Live
Active

Transforms raw policy acknowledgement data into directional governance risk signals. Distinguishes between minor acknowledgement gaps and critical compliance exposures based on policy criticality tier, workforce coverage percentage, and time since distribution.

Classification Logic
Policy criticality tier (1–3) Coverage % (acknowledged/assigned) Days since distribution Role criticality weight
Data protection policy · 95% coverage · 3 days→ Low Risk Signal
H&S policy · 72% coverage · 12 days→ Amber Alert
Critical compliance · 40% coverage · 21 days→ Governance Exposure
